Luna's Quest

About the Game

My game is about a young fairy named Luna that embarks on an epic quest to save her sister. She is being possessed by the an evil Dryad, named Zinnia. She wants the Opal Stone that she will combined with hers, The Tourmaline Stone. But the stone was given to Luna and was told to protect it with her life. So how can she stop Zinnia from possessing her sister? Find out when the game is released. Can you complete the quest?

Game Play

In this epic quest to free your sister from the evil grasp of Zinnia, you will have to fight your way though dangerous enemies, challenging problems and puzzles and extreme boss fights!

When faced with the puzzles they could either be simple jump puzzles or complex mind puzzles. What ever they are you will always need to complete them or you wont be aloud to proceed to the next level.

Characters

In this amazing you will encounter main charters. The main characters include Luna, Hazel, Zinnia, The Minotaura and The Chimera. These are the main characters because three of them are bosses and the other two are just the main charaters that the game will focus mostly on.
